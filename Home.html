Get To Now Me Better

I am  Mohamed Mostafa, and my friends call me  Mando  :"

I am an enthusiast passionate about Art, Computer Science, and Entrepreneurship. I aspire to work and innovate in the bioinformatics discipline as it integrates my most beloved fields of interest. I assume it is my perfect fit. 

For now, I am a senior student at STEM High School for Boys - 6th of October after being selected among the brightest 175 Egyptian students in the country to attend this innovative boarding school. I am working on a separate capstone project every semester to solve one of Egypt's grand challenges in addition to studying a STEM-focused Honors curriculum. 

Besides studying, I am growing my passion for CS and Entrepreneurship through seeking more opportunities, being involved, and getting my hands-on experience. 

In the next few years, I am looking forward to pursuing my higher education in the US, somewhere that constitutes a community that fits my aspirations, helps me achieve them, and allows me to reach out to mysterious realms of knowledge.
